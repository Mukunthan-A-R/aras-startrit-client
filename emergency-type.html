After pressing SOS: Instructions to communicate with responders

  • Clear any nearby obstacles that may impede access to you and your location.
  • Stay as visible as possible, utilizing bright clothing or items to attract attention.
  • When help arrives, communicate clearly: Who you are, what happened, and where you are, plus any critical health concerns.
  • Follow the instructions given by the responders carefully.
